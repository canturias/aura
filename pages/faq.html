What makes Aura smartphones different?

Our phones feature ultra fast processors, powerful cameras, and seamless intergration with our End-to-End cloud storage for automatic backups and syncing.

What operating system do your phones use?

Our phones use AuraOS, an optimized system based on Android, with added privacy and security.

Do Aura phones support 5G?

Yes! All our phones (except the Low and Lite models) are 5G-compatible for the fastest connectivity available.

How much cloud storage do I get with my phone?

Every Aura Phone comes with 16 GB of free cloud storage. Additional plans are available for purchase.

Is my data secure in Aura Cloud?

Of course! Your files are secured with End-to-End Encryption to keep your file safe and private. So private, even we can't see them!

Can I customize my Minecraft Server?

Yes! You have full control over mods, plugins, datapacks, and world settings.

Do I need technical knowledge to run a server?

Not at all! Our user-friendly setup and management dashboard make it easy for anyone to host a server.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and recently cryptocurrency payments.
